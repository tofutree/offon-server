Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

off/on ("we", "us") operates offon.app. This policy explains how we collect and use your information.

Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your name, handle, phone number, and device type. When you post via MMS or email, we store the message content and any attached images.

How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number solely to link incoming MMS messages to your account. We send only one outbound message per post: a delivery confirmation ("✓ posted to off/on"). We do not send marketing messages.

SMS/MMS Messaging

By registering, you consent to receive SMS/MMS confirmation messages from off/on. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your phone number is never displayed publicly. Public posts are visible to all visitors; private posts are visible only to approved followers.
